## Context
Royal letter from Charles II confirming jurisdiction to Rhode Island. John Greene appears as Deputy alongside Randall Holden, certifying the colony's claim. This record confirms/re-enters an earlier 1664 Act of Voluntary Submission of the Chief Sachem.

## Limitations
- Does not identify Joan Greene.
- "Forty years" claim is a jurisdictional argument, not neutral chronology.
- No Indigenous signatories or individuals named in this excerpt.
