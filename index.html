The Anomaly — Two Appearances

Why does a woman who appears twice have to give consent?

March 24, 1681/2 (OS) — John & Joan convey home-place to sons Daniel & James, reserving 30s life estate to Joan, "or to her mother if she survive." May 19, 1682 — John & Joan give "free & voluntary consent" to William Carpenter, Pawtuxet. Witnesses: John Wickes, Samuel Gorton.

She is alive at both. English law reached for her twice in 56 days because her title was load-bearing. Then she disappears. No death, burial, probate, dower. No record after May 19, 1682.

Where English women should appear, Joan does not. See English proof comparison below — Sarah Stanton, Mary Tibbitts, Elizabeth Wickes appear in multiple record types. Joan appears only when land title required her.
